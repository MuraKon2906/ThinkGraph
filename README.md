# Overview

ThinkGraph helps users deeply understand complex academic papers, research documents, and technical reports.

Instead of generating shallow summaries, ThinkGraph converts dense information into structured markdown documents with connected subtopics, contextual explanations, and intelligent research navigation.

The platform is designed for:
- Researchers
- Students
- Engineers
- Developers
- Technical teams
- Curious learners

ThinkGraph transforms static documents into interactive learning experiences.

# Why ThinkGraph Is Different

Most AI research tools stop at summarization.

ThinkGraph focuses on **comprehension**.

It combines:
- AI-powered document analysis
- Structured markdown generation
- Topic relationship mapping
- Research exploration workflows

The result is a system that helps users understand *why* concepts matter and *how* they connect — not just what the paper says.

Instead of reading papers line-by-line and manually researching unfamiliar concepts, users get an interactive research graph that continuously expands understanding.

### Pipeline Overview
1. User uploads a research paper or report
2. ThinkGraph extracts and analyzes content
3. AI pipelines generate structured markdown
4. Related concepts and subtopics are discovered
5. Knowledge relationships are linked dynamically
6. Users explore concepts interactively
